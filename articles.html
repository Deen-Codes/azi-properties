Straightforward mortgage guidance for buyers, homeowners and investors

A library of clear, practical articles from AZI Mortgages — covering deposits, affordability, remortgaging, buy-to-let, moving home and specialist lending across the UK market.

Plain-English answers to the questions that actually matter

Mortgage decisions are rarely about rate alone. They're about affordability, structure, timing and the criteria of the right lender for your situation. Our articles are written to help you cut through the noise and understand the decisions in front of you.

Each piece is written for real UK applicants — first-time buyers working out deposits, homeowners planning a move, investors building a portfolio, and anyone with an income or property that doesn't fit the high-street mould.
