For the purposes of the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018, AZI Mortgages is the "data controller" for information collected through this website and through our advice process.

1. Information we collect

When you contact us or use our enquiry forms, we may collect:

  • Contact details (name, email address, phone number and postal address)
  • Financial information relevant to your mortgage enquiry (such as income, employment status, deposit, existing mortgage details and credit commitments)
  • Information you share in messages, consultation calls or documents you upload
  • Technical information collected automatically when you visit the site (such as IP address, browser type, pages viewed and cookies — see our Cookie Policy)

2. How we use your information

We process personal information for the following purposes:

  • Responding to enquiries and arranging consultations
  • Providing mortgage advice that is suitable for your circumstances
  • Researching and submitting applications to lenders on your behalf
  • Meeting our legal and regulatory obligations, including those set by the Financial Conduct Authority
  • Keeping records of the advice we give (required for at least the minimum period set out by FCA rules)
  • Improving the website and the quality of the service we provide

3. Legal basis for processing

Depending on the context, we rely on one or more of the following lawful bases under UK GDPR:

  • Performance of a contract — when we are providing advice or arranging a product you've asked us to recommend
  • Legitimate interests — to run our business, respond to enquiries and maintain appropriate records
  • Legal obligation — to comply with regulatory, tax and anti-money-laundering requirements
  • Consent — for optional marketing communications (you can withdraw consent at any time)

4. Who we share your information with

We only share personal information where it's necessary to provide the service you've asked for, to comply with the law, or with a processor acting under our instructions. This can include:

  • Mortgage lenders and product providers considered for your case
  • Credit reference and fraud prevention agencies
  • Our regulatory, compliance and professional advisers
  • IT, CRM and email providers acting as data processors on our behalf
  • Legal, regulatory or government bodies where required by law

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

5. International transfers

Our usual processing takes place in the UK. Where a supplier processes personal information outside the UK, we put in place appropriate safeguards (such as UK International Data Transfer Agreements or equivalent approved mechanisms).

6. How long we keep information

We keep records for as long as needed to deliver our service and to meet legal and regulatory retention requirements. For regulated advice, this is typically at least the minimum period required by FCA rules (commonly several years after the advice is given), and longer where required by lenders or law.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask for inaccurate data to be corrected
  • Request erasure of data in certain circumstances
  • Restrict or object to processing in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw consent where we rely on it
  • Request data portability where applicable
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk

8. Security

We put in place technical and organisational measures appropriate to the sensitivity of the information we handle, including access controls, encryption in transit, secure document handling and staff training.

9. Children's data

Our services are aimed at adults. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child's data has been shared with us, please contact us and we'll remove it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated "last updated" date.
