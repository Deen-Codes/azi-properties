Cookie Policy

This page explains what cookies and similar technologies AZI Mortgages uses, why we use them, and how you can control them.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They're widely used to make websites work, remember preferences, and provide information to the site owner. Similar technologies include pixels, local storage and tracking scripts — we refer to all of these collectively as "cookies" in this policy.

2. The cookies we use

[PLACEHOLDER — REVIEW BEFORE LAUNCH] The specific cookies active on the site depend on the tools you ultimately connect (analytics, CRM, form providers, video embeds, chat widgets, etc.). The categories below describe the types we're likely to use.

Strictly necessary cookies

These are required for the site to function — for example, remembering that you've dismissed a cookie banner, or keeping a secure session while you complete an enquiry form. These cookies don't need consent.

Performance & analytics cookies

We may use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or a privacy-focused alternative) to understand how visitors use the site, which pages are most useful and where to improve. Analytics cookies are only set after you've given consent.

Functional cookies

These remember choices you make — for example, a preferred contact option or a saved enquiry progress state. They're set once you've given consent.

Marketing cookies

We may use marketing cookies to measure the effectiveness of advertising or to show relevant content on other sites. These are only set if you've given explicit consent.

3. Third-party cookies

Some cookies are set by third parties we use to provide services (for example, embedded videos, form providers or analytics). Those providers have their own privacy policies and you should review them directly if you want to know more.

4. How to manage cookies

You can control cookies in two ways:

  • Through our cookie banner — when present, it allows you to accept, reject or customise non-essential cookies.
  • Through your browser — most browsers let you view, delete and block cookies. Refer to your browser's help pages or visit aboutcookies.org for general guidance.

Blocking strictly necessary cookies may break parts of the site.

5. Changes to this policy

If we change the cookies or technologies used on the site, we'll update this page and, where required, ask for your consent again.

6. Contact us

For questions about this cookie policy, contact privacy@aziproperties.co.uk.
